Homeboy Moves to the City!

After an incredible 12 months in Hahndorf, Homeboy is stepping into the heart of Adelaide's bustling CBD! Nestled under the Yugo student accommodation building, right across from Adelaide University, Homeboy is ready to bring its signature good vibes, great coffee, and irresistible focaccia to a brand-new crowd.

With a vision to create a close-knit community, Tom, the driving force behind Homeboy, is excited to foster connections over takeaway coffees and delicious sandos throughout the week. Thanks to Renew Adelaide, this once-empty space is transforming into the coffee shop of his dreams.

How Homeboy found his spot!

Season 1 - Thiele’s Shop

Ever wondered how someone can start a café at just 18 years old? It’s a great question, and the answer lies in an incredible opportunity. Season 1 of Homeboy wouldn’t have been possible without the collaboration of Thiele’s Shop and The PaintBox, two creative venues that empower young entrepreneurs and artists to test their ideas in a real, brick-and-mortar setting.

Thiele’s Shop operates as a dynamic hub, hosting pop-ups that keep the space fresh and exciting. This unique model allowed Tom to focus entirely on what mattered—crafting amazing coffee, elevating customer experiences, and sharing the journey through media content.
